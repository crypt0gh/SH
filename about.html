About Gardenor

I'm Breno Martins, the founder of the project Sweet Home. With a passion for transforming spaces and a wealth of experience in general services—especially in gardening, landscaping, painting, and finishing—I take pride in delivering high-quality results. Yeah, as you've noteced, i am gardenor...

Who Am I

Over the years, I’ve worked both independently and with major companies like the Multi-Nacional Gerdau, always striving for excellence in every project. With a passion for problem-solving and innovation, I combine my technical expertise with hands-on skills to deliver efficient, high-quality services. I also have a deep interest in languages, speaking Portuguese, and other languages, while actively developing fluency in Russian. My goal is to create and enhance outdoor spaces with professionalism, creativity, and sustainability, always exceeding expectations.

My value

I believe in quality, sustainability, and dedication to every project. My goal is to transform outdoor spaces with care, ensuring beauty, harmony, and long-lasting results. Every garden deserves attention, and I am committed to delivering excellence with integrity and passion.

My mission

My mission at Sweet Home Project is to transform and enhance outdoor spaces by providing exceptional landscaping and gardening services. I strive to combine creativity, quality, and sustainability in every project, ensuring that each space I work on is not only beautiful but also healthy and well-maintained. I am dedicated to offering personalized services that meet the unique needs of every client, delivering results that exceed expectations while fostering a deep respect for nature. Ultimately, my goal is to help people create outdoor environments where they can relax, enjoy, and connect with nature—whether it's a small garden, a commercial space, or a large landscaping project.

My vision

My vision for Sweet Home is to create a company that not only transforms outdoor spaces but also builds lasting relationships with clients, grounded in trust, quality, and care. I see Sweet Home growing into a team of dedicated professionals, all committed to delivering top-tier gardening, landscaping, and maintenance services, with a strong focus on sustainability and innovation. I aspire to expand my reach across multiple regions, bringing beautiful, functional, and eco-friendly green spaces to both residential and commercial clients. Ultimately, I want Sweet Home to be a leader in the industry, known for its personalized service and unwavering commitment to excellence.
