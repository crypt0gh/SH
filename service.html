Our service

At Sweet Home, we offer complete solutions for the maintenance and enhancement of green spaces. Whether for homes, businesses, or public areas, we ensure high-quality service with specialized techniques and respect for nature. Our commitment is to transform and care for your environment with efficiency and professionalism, bringing beauty, harmony, and well-being.

Lawn Clean

Clearing fallen leaves, branches, and other waste to keep your lawn neat.

Garden Help

Eco-friendly solutions to protect your plants from harmful pests and diseases.

Garden Care

Mowing, trimming, and weed removal to keep your grass lush and green.

Nutrient-rich treatments to promote strong and healthy plant development.

Green Space Care

From lawns and ornamental plants to trees and even aquatic gardens, we provide specialized care with expert techniques.

Garden Creation & Landscaping

We design and implement projects that blend aesthetics and functionality, making your environment more beautiful and harmonious.

Garden Maintenance

We take care of pruning, fertilization, pest control, and everything your garden needs to stay healthy and vibrant.
